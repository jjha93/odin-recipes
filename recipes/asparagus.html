Grilled Asparagus

Description

This mouthwatering grilled asparagus will be a hit at your next get together! It's simple ad easy to make and the perfect addition to a barbeque. It takes only a few ingredients and a few minutes and it's ready to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3-5 minutes.
